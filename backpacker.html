The Flaming Kiwi Backpackers

Close to the town centre but on a quiet and mercifully hill-free street, this friendly hostel offers tidy dorms with a locker for every bed, three kitchens, unlimited wi-fi and a bottle of sunblock at reception. An excellent choice, then.

Southern Laughter Backpackers

It offer a great stay in a great location and it's just a short walk to all the attractions, bars, shops and restaurants. Enjoy free off street parking, free hot veggie soup every night, free spa pool, free luggage storage, and drinks discounts all around town. All of our rooms come fully made up with linen and duvets, and most of the dorms include en-suite and/or kitchen facilities. It also have two big kitchens and TV lounges to relax in (free DVDs available), free unlimited WIFI, and two sunny BBQ decks with amazing views of the Remarkables mountain range.

Adventure Q2 Hostel

Catering to young independent travellers from around the world it offer mostly shared room accommodation and run in-house activities most nights a week to make it easy to meet new people from around the world. The beds are custom designed timber bunks with comfortable innerspring mattresses. The Village Green is right across the street to enjoy a drink in the sun, our common areas are large for a small hostel which allows plenty of room to engage with fellow travellers. Located in the very centre of Queenstown, you're a 2-3min walk to everything, including the Village Green just across the street.

Absoloot Value Accommodation

Absoloot Value Accommodation (AVA) opened in June 2010 with a variety of accommodation options for the backpacker, flash-packer and value-seeking traveler! AVA has an outlook second-to-none and four stories worth of character and comfort. It provide free linen and towels for all guests, and every bathroom includes hair and body wash for everyone's use. Each room has been fitted with double glazing and shutters to keep the heat in and the noise out. And there's a heat pump/reverse-cycle air-conditioner in every room, so customer can stay warm in winter and cool in summer.
