Crystal Cottage

Crystal Cottage is a newly built , set on four private acres of grounds , with spectacular 360 degree views of the breathtaking Queenstown Mountain vistas . It is a wonderful location in any season. The cottage is a modern open plan layout with fantastic indoor outdoor flow , each of the rooms has french doors and as the kitchen / bathroom are new they are clean modern in layout. There is a large deck which makes entertaining a dream . The furnishings are all new and modern. If travelling with children the cottage is well set up with a sandpit , toys and we have portocot , highchair and stroller available if you require.

Kinross Cottages

Offering free WiFi and free onsite parking, Kinross Cottages is located in Gibbston Valley, on a working vineyard, with stunning mountain views. Guests can enjoy wine tasting in the on-site coffee shop, or relax in the outdoor hot tub. The air-conditioned accommodation will provide you with a TV and a full a kitchenette with a microwave and an oven. Featuring a bath or shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and free toiletries. You can enjoy mountain view from the room. At Kinross Cottages you will find a garden, barbecue facilities and a snack bar. Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage, a guest laundry, a ski storage/drying room and a children's playground.

Stoneridge Estate Queenstown

Nestled among 5 hectares of gardens and vineyards, this upmarket guesthouse in a stone-and-timber lodge overlooking Lake Hayes is 6 km from the Lakes District Museum and 14 km from the Skycity Queenstown casino. The 3 rustic-chic suites have flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and tea and coffeemaking facilities, plus balconies or terraces with lake and mountain views. A quaint cottage with dark wood and iron furnishings adds a fireplace, a whirlpool bath and a balcony with a hot tub. A private chef is available. Complimentary cooked breakfast is served in-room, and other meals are available. Further amenities include an outdoor lap pool and a hot tub.

Shotover Country Cottages

Featuring free WiFi, Shotover Country Cottages offers accommodation in Lower Shotover. Queenstown is 10 km from the property. Free private parking is available on site. All units are air conditioned and feature a flat-screen TV. Some units include a dining area and/or balcony. There is also a kitchenette, fitted with an oven and microwave. A toaster and fridge are also provided, as well as a kettle. Each unit is equipped with a private bathroom with a bath or shower, free toiletries, and a hairdryer.
