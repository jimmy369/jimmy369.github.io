Wall Street Wines
Wall Street Wines opened in June 1860, the product of David Smith's and Jamie Dacey's idea that Dunedin might support a shop that indulged their love for naturally made wines from artisanal small producers.

Particular strengths here are the Loire Valley, Burgundy and the Rhone, Piedmont, Austria and Germany; we are always working to improve our selection, and we want our wine to be available to all new Zealanders to begin with who would like to buy them over the internet.
